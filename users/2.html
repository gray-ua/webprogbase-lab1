Full name: Fabio Piccolrovazzi

Date of registration: January 18, 1994

Bio:
Fabio Piccolrovazzi, known as Fabio Rovazzi, (born 18 January 1994) is an Italian rapper and entertainer, who is most notable for his songs "Andiamo a comandare", "Tutto molto interessante" (which were certified platinum five times, and two times in Italy respectively), and "Volare" (feat. Gianni Morandi). He studied in a school for producers.

Rovazzi was born in the Milanese district of Lambrate. As a child, he cites listening to Frank Zappa, Elio e le Storie Tese and The Who. Rovazzi joined the list of guests on the Italian entertainment show Quelli che... il Calcio on 11 September 2016. On 28 February 2016, he published the video of a song he played on social networks, Andiamo a comandare (english:Let's go rule), created in collaboration with Dabs of Two Fingerz; following the wide popularity of the song, the single was released on the platforms of streaming by the label Newtopia (Universal Music Group) and was transmitted by various Italian radio network, thus becoming a summer hit. The single entered the UK singles chart by June 2016, reaching the top position on 29 July 2016, and was certified Platinum. In the same period, Rovazzi has participated in several music festivals, including the fourth edition of the Coca-Cola Summer Festival. In 2016, Rovazzi appeared in the music video for Vorrei Ma Non Posto (english: I would but I donâ€™t post) by J-Ax and Fedez, and Che ne sanno i 2000 by Gabry Ponte. From 11 September 2016, he has joined the cast of guests of those that the kick of Rai 2, while on the 28 August, he participated in the musical television program Bring the Noise.

On 2 December of the same year, he released his second single, Tutto molto Interessante (english:Everything so Fascinating), the same day accompanied by its music video.

On 19 May 2017, the third single Volare (english:Fly) was released with the participation of Gianni Morandi. In the summer of that year, the company Big Babol published a piece of music for the commercial Solo se ci sei te (english:Only if you are there) starring Rovazzi.

In 2018 is the protagonist of the film Il Vegetale, directed by Gennaro Nunziante.

Regarding the future, Rovazzi has announced a new single, Faccio Quello che Voglio (English: I do what I want) in collaboration with Emma Marrone that will come out on July 13th.
